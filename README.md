1. Objective : 

The objective of this project was to create a complete and professional brand identity for a firm. The project focused on building a modern, trustworthy, and visually consistent brand presence across different marketing and business materials.

2. Steps Performed :
   a. Designed a unique and minimal logo identity
   b. Selected a professional colour palette and typography style
   c. Created business card designs (front and back)
   d. Designed social media promotional posts for online branding
   e. Developed a professional marketing banner for promotions
   f. Maintained consistency in layout, colours, and branding across all creatives

3. Tools Used : 
   a. Adobe Photoshop
   b. Adobe Illustrator

4. Outcome :

The final project successfully delivers a strong and premium brand identity that reflects professionalism, trust, and financial expertise. The complete branding system creates a clean and modern visual appearance suitable for business promotions, client communication, and digital marketing.
